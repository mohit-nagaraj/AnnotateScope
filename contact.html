Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on AnnotateScope?

To create an account on AnnotateScope, visit the website and click the "GET STARTED" button. Follow the registration process to create your account.

Can I access AnnotateScope on mobile devices?

Yes, AnnotateScope is accessible on mobile devices. You can use it on smartphones and tablets for a flexible learning experience.

Are my annotations private?

By default, your annotations are private. You can choose to share them with specific users or make them public if you prefer.

How do I contact AnnotateScope support?

You can reach our support team by emailing support@annotatescope.com or using the contact form on the website.

What types of microscopy slides are available on AnnotateScope?

AnnotateScope offers a wide range of microscopy slides covering various subjects, including biology, pathology, and more. You can explore the available options in the slide menu.

Is there a community forum for users to discuss their experiences and share tips?

Yes, we have a dedicated community forum where users can engage in discussions, share experiences, and exchange tips and tricks related to AnnotateScope. You can access it through the "Community" section.
